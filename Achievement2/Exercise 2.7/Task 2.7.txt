I think users would want to search for recipes, recipes with a certain ingredient, and the difficulty level.

I think users will want to search for recipes for a certain ingredient because if they know they want chicken, they can look up all of the chicken recipes.

I think users will want to search for the level of difficulty of recipes because they might look for dishes that are easy to make.

Data Analysis:
For the bar chart and the line chart, the x label is the name and the y label is the cook time.
For the pie chart, the cook time is the label and the name is in the legend.

Execution Flow:
Home Page -> Loggin -> Recipes List -> All Recipes -> Search -> Look at charts -> Check Details -> Go Back -> Log out
